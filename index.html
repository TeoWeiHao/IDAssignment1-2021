Biography

Rain Drops is a music unit belonging to Nijisanji including 6 virtual livers; Ryushen, Saegusa Akina, Warabeda Meiji, Suzuki Masaru, Elu and Joe Rikiichi.

During the "Virtual to LIVE" event held by Nijisanji on 8th December 2019, Rain Drops debuted under the music label "Virgin Music"

On 23 January 2020, the members of the music unit were announced in a livestream on the official Nijisanji YouTube channel. This event reached No. 1 trending on Twitter and the livestream had reached over 51 thousand live viewers.

 

Their first MINI album "Synesthesia" was released on 13 May 2020. This album reached the first place in a total of 11 categories, including the Oricon Weekly Total Ranking and it attained the ranking of "Hot Albums" by Billboard JAPAN.

 

A second MINI album "Ontology" was released on 25 Nov 2020.

On 14 Apr 2021, a SINGLE "Refrains" was released as a GOODS CD.
